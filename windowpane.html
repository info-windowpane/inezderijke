Windowpane, 2025

Windowpane is a space for curatorial and material experiment which re-frames postcards as exhibition matter. Projects are concerned with light, display (and concealment), puzzles, and communication. Each project is made of twenty postcards, mounted as a window display. More information can be found at: windowpane.eu
