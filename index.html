Boomark book, 2025

In The Carrier Bag Theory of Fiction, K. Le Guin proposes that authors should seek collective histories (gatherers) over solo-heroes (hunters). Bookmark Book explores this theory through physical form by inviting you tear out each page as you finish reading it. The pages and their teaching can be endlessly and randomly shared via dissemination into new books as bookmarks.

Documentation Josh Croll. Orders and inquiries can be placed by email.

Windowpane, Re-toured, 2025

In an off-site installment of Windowpane, five postcards are suspended from a steel mount so as to allow the show-through of the paper to reveal both sides of the images.

Each card represents a layer of Dutch landscape as seen from the train, and their hanging form mimics that of a train carriage.
